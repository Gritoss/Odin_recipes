BIRIYANI

DESCRIPTION

Biriyani, a spicy chicken and rice dish, is one of the most well-known Indian-Pakistani dishes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oiL
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 small green chile peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

PROCEDURE

  1. Marinate Chicken – Mix 500g chicken with ½ cup yogurt, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Rest for 1 hour.
  2. Cook Rice – Boil 6 cups water with salt, cloves, cardamom, bay leaf, and cinnamon. Add 2 cups soaked basmati rice. Cook 80%, then drain.
  3. Prepare Masala – Fry 2 sliced onions in oil & ghee until golden. Add 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 2 chopped tomatoes, green chilies, and spices. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  4. Layer & Dum Cook – In a pot, layer rice and chicken. Top with saffron milk, ghee, fried onions, and herbs. Cover and cook on low for 20-25 mins.
  5. Serve & Enjoy – Mix gently and serve hot with raita! 🍛✨
